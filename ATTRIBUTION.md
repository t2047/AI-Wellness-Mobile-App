### Usage

The MSD Manuals content is used in this project solely for **educational and non-commercial research purposes** as part of a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipeline. The content is:

- Scraped responsibly in accordance with the site's `robots.txt` (see `scraper/robots.txt`)
- Cached locally under `scraper/output/` for offline indexing
- Used exclusively as a knowledge base for the AI agent's RAG system
